FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a website take to build?
A single-page website typically takes 3–5 days. A 5-page business site takes 1–2 weeks. Timelines are agreed upfront with every project brief.
Do you work with clients outside Kashmir?
Yes — we work with clients across India and internationally. All communication happens async via WhatsApp, email, or video call depending on your preference.
Can I get a custom quote for something not listed?
Absolutely. Send a brief via the contact page or WhatsApp and we'll scope the project and send a transparent quote within 24 hours.
How do payments work?
Most projects begin with 50% upfront. The remaining 50% is collected before final delivery. Monthly retainers are billed at the start of each cycle.
Do you offer post-launch support?
Yes. We offer maintenance packages and are available for updates, fixes, and improvements after launch. We don't ghost after delivery.
What do you need to get started?
A brief — even rough notes, a voice message, or a quick description of what you need is enough. We'll handle the scoping and planning from there.
