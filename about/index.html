Tron Develops was founded by Uzaid Bhat with one clear goal: give businesses a digital presence that actually works — not just looks good. What started as a one-person operation in Srinagar has grown into a full-service agency with a dedicated team of specialists, each owning their craft.

Our team covers the full digital stack — from strategy and development to design, content, and growth marketing. Every project gets the right person for the job, not a generalist trying to do everything at once. That's how we ship work we're proud to put our name on.

We've worked with international nonprofits, local tech firms, and growing businesses across India and beyond. Our roots are in Kashmir — our reach isn't. Whether you're launching, scaling, or repositioning — we build digital infrastructure that grows with you.
